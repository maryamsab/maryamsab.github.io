User Exprience Research





Currently, I am working as a Sr Design Experience Researcher.

I work closely with a large network of strategists, designers, product managers, developers, and business partners, in agile environments, to extract and prioritize stakeholders needs, project goals, and users characteristics.

I am leading several studies by crafting research questions, designing the approach and recruitment plan, and developing innovative rigorous methods and tools to collect data, Techniques: remote/in-lab usability studies, user interviews, A/B testing, tree testing, contextual inquiry, and ethnography; Tools: Eye tracker, Empatica, Emotion recognition software packages; Optimalworkshop, Surveygizmo, Morae..

I utilize Adobe Analytics tool and internal tools on customers' search queries and error reports to inform my research decisions.

After running the studies, I utilize various range of qualitative/ quantitative approaches to interpret the collected data and translate findings into easily understandable insights used to drive ideation and inform the design.

Collaboratively with the design team, we propose design recommendations to the stakeholders to enable exceptional user experience for customers and employees across banking, investing, and insurance platforms of Canada and U.S.
